Three Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • Base:
    • A packet of cookies.
    • A tablespoon of melted butter
  • Black Coat:
    • 250 g dark chocolate for desserts.
    • A sachet of curd
    • 200ml milk
    • 200ml cream
  • Brown Layer:
    • 250 g milk chocolate.
    • A sachet of curd
    • 200ml milk
    • 200ml cream
  • White Coat:
    • 250 g white chocolate.
    • A sachet of curd
    • 200ml milk
    • 200ml cream

Elaboration

  1. First of all, prepare the base: Crush the biscuits with the chopped butter. Pour it into a springform mold, spreading it very well all over its base, and put it in the oven for a few minutes to get hard and crispy.
  2. Heat the milk and cream, add the chopped dark chocolate and when it is dissolved, add the curd... Leave it over a low heat, stirring non-stop for 7 minutes, making sure it does not boil. It is then followed by Tip over the biscuit base.
  3. Then do the same with the milk chocolate and pour it on top of the dark chocolate. To prevent a hole from being made over the previous mixture, break the cascade by dropping the liquid onto a spoon. And then you do the same thing with white chocolate...
